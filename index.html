Interview #1: Merrill Provost, Aims McGuinness

Interviewer: Hello.

Aims: Hello.

Interviewer: What would you say is the most important thing about Merrill?

Aims: I think the most important thing about Merrill is it’s willingness to accept other cultures. It’s truly amazing that we have such a unique and decdicated group of students collected in one place on top of the hill here at UCSC.
